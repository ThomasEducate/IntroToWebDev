Restaurant Description
Welcome to Tommy's Eatery! Home of simple plates and great eats! Our restaurant was founded back in 1984 and has been serving the local community as an institution ever since. We feature a small variety of items available during the lunch hours Monday thru Friday. We also have available for reservation the highly reconmended party room. Great for your next gathering of friends! New in 2019, we will now be providing catering options to bring you Tommy's institutional favorites to you!
